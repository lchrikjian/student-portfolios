Insight:
AI in Robotics involves integrating artificial intelligence with physical machines. This field utilizes computer vision, machine learning, and reinforcement learning. 
First introduced at the 1956 Dartmouth Conference, AI in robotics has found numerous applications in various industries.
Python and C++ are the two main programming languages for AI in robotics, with ROS being the most widely used middleware framework.
