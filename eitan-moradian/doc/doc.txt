This presentation provides an overview of the most important technologies shaping the future of computing and artificial intelligence. It covers the foundations of AI and Machine Learning, how data science and big data analytics drive modern decision-making, and a side-by-side comparison of today's leading large language models — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. From there, it explores how AI is being applied in robotics and the real world, introduces the concept of AI agents and autonomous tools like Claude Code, and breaks down CUDA — the GPU computing platform that makes large-scale AI training possible. By the end, the audience will have a clear, connected picture of how these technologies relate to each other and why they matter.
